What is DreamWed?

DreamWed is the place you need to be when planning your dream wedding. Weddings are a joyous and memorable moment but can be very stressful to plan, but worry no more dreamwed is here! DreamWed allows you to connect to wedding vendors such as florists, wedding venues, tailors, photographers etc. As if that's not enough we provide tools such as budgeting and guestlist managers that help you along planning your dream day! What are you waiting for? Let's make that dream wedding a reality!

For Vendors
